What We Do

The Research Society is a collaborative research club where members work together to write and publish research papers to professional and high school journals.

Research Topics

Topics are decided by members and may include:


  • Medicine
  • Life Sciences
  • Computer Science & AI
  • Engineering
  • Other fields based on member interests

Why You Should Join

Build Your Portfolio

Gain hands-on research experience regardless of your major (medicine, computer science, or other fields)

Resume Excellence

Add published research to your academic profile and develop valuable research skills

Collaborative Learning

Work with peers on professional research methodologies and academic writing
